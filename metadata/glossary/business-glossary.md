# Business Glossary

## 1. Overview
The Business Glossary is a centralized dictionary defining the standard vocabulary used across the CUHP Question Bank platform. It eliminates ambiguity and ensures everyone speaks the same language.

## 2. Core Vocabularies

### 2.1 Academic Terms
* **Course**: A specific unit of teaching (e.g., "Intro to Programming").
* **Subject**: A broader domain of knowledge.
* **Academic Session**: The operational time period (e.g., "Fall 2026").

### 2.2 Repository Terms
* **Question Paper**: A validated document containing examination questions.
* **Moderation**: The workflow of reviewing a Question Paper for quality before publication.

### 2.3 AI Platform Terms
* **Vector**: A numerical representation of text used for similarity search.
* **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)**: The process of retrieving relevant Question Papers to ground LLM responses.
* **Token**: The fundamental unit of AI cost computation.

### 2.4 Governance Terms
* **Data Owner**: The executive accountable for a dataset's accuracy.
* **Data Steward**: The individual responsible for day-to-day data quality management.
* **PII (Personally Identifiable Information)**: Data that can identify an individual (e.g., Student Email, IP Address).

## 3. Integration with the Catalog
When viewing a dataset in the Metadata Catalog, hovering over a column name (e.g., `academic_session_id`) displays a tooltip pulling the definition directly from this Business Glossary.
