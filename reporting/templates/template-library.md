# Report Template Library

## 1. Overview
The Template Library contains standardized, pre-built reports that cover 80% of common analytical use cases. Users can clone a template and customize it.

## 2. Core Templates

### 2.1 Academic Reports
* **Curriculum Gap Analysis**: Details which courses lack uploaded question papers for the current academic session.
* **Subject Popularity Report**: Cross-references subject uploads against student download counts.

### 2.2 Repository Reports
* **Moderation Queue Status**: A tabular report of all papers currently in `IN_REVIEW`.
* **Upload Audit Log**: A detailed history of who uploaded what and when.

### 2.3 AI Platform Reports
* **Token Usage by Department**: Financial breakdown of AI costs aggregated by academic department.
* **Top AI Queries**: List of the most frequent search terms/questions asked to the Copilot.

### 2.4 Operations & Executive Reports
* **System Uptime & Error Rate**: Technical SLA compliance report.
* **Executive Summary**: Monthly digest of DAU/MAU, total content growth, and active storage.
