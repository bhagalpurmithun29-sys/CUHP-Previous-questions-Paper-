# Board Member User Guide

## 1. Welcome to the Executive Portal
The CUHP Question Bank Executive Portal provides you with a high-level, real-time overview of institutional performance, academic excellence, and operational health.

## 2. Navigating Dashboards
* **University Overview**: Start here. This dashboard provides a blended view of the most critical KPIs across the university.
* **Drill-down**: If you see a metric that concerns you (e.g., "Moderation Speed is Red"), simply click on the metric. The platform will "drill down" and show you a breakdown by Department, allowing you to identify the bottleneck instantly.

## 3. Accessing Board Reports
* **Download Center**: Navigate to the `Board Reports` section in the sidebar to download your scheduled Quarterly and Annual reports.
* **Executive Briefings**: Check your portal inbox or email for automated weekly Briefings. These are AI-generated, plain-english summaries of the university's performance for the week.

## 4. Strategic Scorecards
* View the **Institutional Scorecard** to track progress against the Vice Chancellor's strategic goals. 
* The scorecard balances Academic, Financial, and Operational perspectives to ensure holistic growth.
