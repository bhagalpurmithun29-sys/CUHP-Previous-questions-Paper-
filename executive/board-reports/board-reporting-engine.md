# Board Reporting Engine

## 1. Overview
The Board Reporting Engine automates the generation of presentation-ready outputs (PDF, PowerPoint) required for institutional governance meetings, saving hundreds of hours of manual compilation.

## 2. Report Types
* **Quarterly Reports**: Standardized digest of the previous 3 months. Highlights achievements against strategic goals.
* **Annual Reports**: Comprehensive year-in-review, focusing on YoY growth, Financial/FinOps summaries, and high-level academic success metrics.
* **Strategic Summaries**: Ad-hoc briefs generated to support a specific board decision (e.g., "AI Investment ROI Brief").

## 3. Automation & Briefings
* **Automated Briefings**: NLP generation (powered by the AI Platform) summarizes the visual charts into readable paragraphs. (e.g., "The Question Bank saw a 20% increase in uploads this quarter, driven largely by the Science Department.")
* **Scheduled Executive Reports**: Generated on the 1st of every month and securely emailed or pushed to the `Board User App`.
