# Scorecard & Risk Framework

## 1. Balanced Scorecards
The Executive Platform uses the Balanced Scorecard methodology to track Institutional Performance Targets across four perspectives:
1. **Academic** (e.g., Curriculum Coverage)
2. **Financial** (e.g., AI API Costs)
3. **Internal Process** (e.g., Moderation Speed)
4. **Learning & Growth** (e.g., AI Copilot Adoption Rate)

## 2. Scorecard Cascading
* **Institutional Scorecard**: The highest level, viewed by the Vice Chancellor.
* **Department Scorecard**: Inherits the goals of the institution but filters data only for a specific department. Used by Deans and Department Heads.

## 3. Risk & Opportunity Tracking
The platform maps KPIs to a Risk Heat Map based on thresholds set by the Decision Intelligence engine.
* **Strategic Risks**: Goals continuously missed for >2 quarters.
* **Operational Risks**: Severe technical SLAs missed (e.g., 99.9% uptime failure).
* **Emerging Opportunities**: Automatic detection of positive outliers (e.g., "Student usage of AI Copilot is 50% higher than expected, consider reallocating budget to support it.").
