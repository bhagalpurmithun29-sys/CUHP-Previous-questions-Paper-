# Game Day Execution Guide

## Planning
Game Days schedule monthly scenarios vetted by the Platform Operations Team.

## Roles
- **Commander**: Executes the faults.
- **Observer**: Monitors the Grafana dashboards and notes anomalies.
- **Responder**: Roleplays the on-call engineer unaware of the drill.

## Goal
Validate our `Circuit Breakers`, `Retry Policies`, and `Automatic Recovery` assumptions under load.
